SAFEGUARDING

Safeguarding is essential because it protects the wellbeing, dignity, and safety of individuals, ensuring that everyone, especially those who are vulnerable, are free from harm, abuse, and neglect.

What is Safeguarding? Signs to Watch For Action Steps

What is Safeguarding?

Safeguarding is the process of protecting individuals—especially children and vulnerable adults—from harm, abuse, and neglect.

Signs to Watch For

Look for changes in behaviour, unexplained injuries, withdrawal, fearfulness, or sudden shifts in mood or performance.

Action Steps

If you’re concerned, stay calm, listen, record what you’ve noticed, and report it to the appropriate safeguarding lead.
